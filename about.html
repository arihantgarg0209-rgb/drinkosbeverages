The Beginning

The DrinkoSip Journey

In December 2021, Mr. Vishal Garg had a bold vision — to create beverages that celebrate the rich, bold flavors of India with zero compromise on purity.

And so, in the heart of Yamunanagar, Haryana, DrinkoSip Beverages was born. Starting with our flagship Lucknawi Zeera — a masala jeera drink inspired by the royal kitchens of Lucknow — we quickly became a household name.

The secret? Authentic spices, real lemons, and an unwavering commitment to quality that's earned us zero complaints since day one.

Milestones

Our Journey

Growing year by year, state by state.

2021

The Beginning

DrinkoSip Beverages founded by Mr. Vishal Garg in Yamunanagar, Haryana. Launched Lucknawi Zeera — the masala jeera drink that started it all.

2022

Expanding Horizons

Expanded across Haryana and entered Punjab. Launched Strong Energy Drink. Growing retailer network with zero complaints.

2023

New States, New Reach

Entered Uttar Pradesh and Uttarakhand. Crossed 500+ retail partners across North India.

2024

Himachal & Beyond

Entered Himachal Pradesh — now in 5 states! Launched Banta Lemon Soda and Packaged Water line. Hit 1000+ retailers.

2025–26

Scaling New Heights

New products, new states, new milestones. Building India's most loved beverage brand. The best is yet to come.
